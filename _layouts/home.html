I am a recent graduate from the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign where I studied Electrical Engineering and Computer Science. Starting in the summer of 2015, I will be a full-stack engineer at Comprehend. In the past, I’ve worked at Nexteer Automotive, JPMorgan Chase, and Microsoft with the Office team.

In my free time, I enjoy playing the piano and organ, watching any Chicago sports team, and rating and commenting UIUC bathrooms on IlliniDumps.

The summer before my freshman year in college, I hacked together a blog in PHP and MySQL in order to learn web development. I did learn a lot during that project, but in the end, it didn’t make a whole lot of sense to keep that project up and running. Since then, I’ve also realized it isn’t necessary to reinvent the wheel. I stumbled across Jekyll and Minimal Mistakes and thought it looked cool enough to try out.
